About this blog

Hi, I am Sal! I decided to do this blog to record my experiences in the loop as an office worker. Working in the restaurant industry for over 15 years I was ready for a change. In 2022 I started to work towards that change. The online program 100devs prepared me immensely and in 2023 I was fortunate enough to be accepted into the DPI Tech Apprenticeship program. It is a 3 month long paid program where I am taught various languages that employers are utilizing. After the 3 months I am placed with a tech company as an apprentice to learn and work hands on for 9 months. Hopefully at that point I am retained as a regular employee. Although I am confident in my abilities, I do not want to take this opportunity for granted. So, I am here to try any foodhall, lunch spot, coffee shop, juice bar, and happy hour bar in and around the loop area. Hopefully this will not just be a 3 month long experience. Come along as I show you the best of what the loop has to offer its workers!
